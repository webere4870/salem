Our Ministries

See the ministries and discipling programs that Salem New Beginnings Church offers

0-5 yrs

We have child care for young children under the age of 5 for new parents, led by approved women in the church

Young Teens

Join our teenage Sunday school after the service for middle school and high schoolers

Men's Discipleship

Join bible studies and book studies, led by pastors and elders on staff

Women's Discipleship

Join bible studies and book studies with other like-minded men in the church, led by women in the church

Family Groups

Join our small family groups where husbands + wives are paired up with other members of the church to grow together through the word

Ministries outside the church

Join other believers in the church as we carry the gospel of Jesus Christ to our local community by addressing its physical and spiritual needs
